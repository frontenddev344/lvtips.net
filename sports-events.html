Sports in Las Vegas.

Something for all sports lovers

NFL Games (Las Vegas Raiders)

  • Venue: Allegiant Stadium
  • Dates: Throughout the NFL season, including home games against notable teams.
  • Highlights: The Raiders' home games promise intense football action and a vibrant atmosphere.

NHL Games (Vegas Golden Knights)

  • Venue: T-Mobile Arena
  • Dates: Home games are scheduled throughout the NHL regular season.
  • Highlights: The Golden Knights continue their quest for dominance with fast-paced hockey and an electric fan experience.

UFC Events

  • Venue: T-Mobile Arena and UFC Apex
  • Dates: Multiple events scheduled over the next six months, including major fight cards.
  • Highlights: Watch high-profile UFC bouts featuring top mixed martial artists in exciting matchups.

NASCAR Cup Series (Las Vegas Motor Speedway)

NBA Summer League

How can I purchase tickets for sports events in Las Vegas?
Tickets for sports events can be purchased through various platforms, including the official websites of the teams and venues, authorized ticket sellers like Ticketmaster, and secondary marketplaces like StubHub. It’s recommended to buy tickets early, especially for high-demand events.
Are there any special packages or deals available for sports fans?
Many venues and hotels offer special packages or deals for sports fans, including accommodations and tickets bundled together. Additionally, some sports events provide VIP packages that include premium seating, exclusive access, and other perks. Check the official event or venue websites for information on available packages and deals.
What are the best times of year to visit Las Vegas for sports events?
Las Vegas hosts major sports events year-round, but peak times include the NFL season (September through February), NHL season (October through April), NASCAR races in March and September, and major boxing matches and UFC events, which can occur throughout the year. The NBA Summer League, held in July, is another popular time for sports fans.
Can I attend multiple sports events during a single trip to Las Vegas?
Yes, Las Vegas offers a packed sports calendar, allowing visitors to attend multiple events during a single trip. With numerous venues located close to each other and frequent event scheduling, you can easily plan to catch several games or matches.
What should I know about parking and transportation for sports events?
Most major sports venues in Las Vegas provide on-site parking, but it can be limited and expensive. Consider using public transportation, ride-sharing services, or staying at a hotel close to the venue. Many hotels offer shuttle services to popular sports venues.
Are there any local sports bars or restaurants to enjoy before or after an event?
Las Vegas is home to numerous sports bars and restaurants where you can enjoy pre- or post- game meals and drinks. Popular spots include Yard House, The Still, and PT’s Pubs. Many of these venues feature large screens and a lively atmosphere, perfect for sports fans.
What are some tips for attending live sports events in Las Vegas?
Arrive early to avoid traffic and secure good parking. Check the venue’s bag policy and prohibited items list before attending. Stay hydrated and wear comfortable clothing, especially if you plan to be on your feet for an extended period. Lastly, be sure to check for any event-specific rules or requirements in advance.
